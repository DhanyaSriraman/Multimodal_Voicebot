FACQA - A Tool to detect Fake news.
Fake news refers to fabricated or misleading information spread through traditional and new media, often with the intent to deceive and manipulate the public. This type of information can be spread deliberately or unintentionally and can harm individuals, organizations, and even nations. It can also create confusion, undermine trust in information sources, and contribute to the spread of misinformation and propaganda. It is important for people to critically evaluate the source and content of news before accepting it as true, and for media outlets and platforms to take steps to combat the spread of fake news.
